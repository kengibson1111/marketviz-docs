# marketviz-docs

Plain-language summary explanations of stock data points and what they mean for investment decisions. Written for the average retail investor.

* [Earnings Metrics](/risk/earnings/) - Earnings growth, quality, stability, EPS analysis, and forward projections
* [Technical Indicators](/risk/technicals/) - Price momentum, volume, volatility, and peer-relative performance
